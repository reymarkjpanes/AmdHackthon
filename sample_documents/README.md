## Built-in Conflicts & Issues (for Clausify to Detect)

These documents intentionally contain **cross-document conflicts** and **internal inconsistencies**:

### Number Conflicts
- **Debt threshold**: Term Sheet says $500K, IRA says $750K
- **Non-compete period**: IRA says 24 months, CEO Employment says 12 months, CTO says 18 months
- **Related-party threshold**: IRA says $100K, Voting Agreement says $150K
- **Headcount**: Financial Projections says 28 FTE, Stock Purchase Agreement says 32 FTE
- **Budget deadline**: Term Sheet says Dec 1, IRA says Nov 15
- **Closing date**: Term Sheet says July 30, SPA says August 1

### Legal/IP Risks
- Unresolved contractor dispute (Alex Rivera, $45K) — affects IP ownership
- Patent deadline language inconsistency (IPA says "may have lapsed" vs DD confirms filed)
- Governing law mismatch (IP Agreement uses California, all others use Delaware)
- Voting Agreement termination date math error (says "10 years" but calculates to 10.5 years)

### Personnel Issues
- David Kim listed in Voting Agreement but not in Term Sheet or IRA as Key Holder
- Revenue concentration risk (top customer = 13.3% of ARR)
